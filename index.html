Healthy Lifestyle Hacks

Start Your Day with Water

Drinking a glass of water first thing in the morning can kickstart your metabolism and help keep you hydrated throughout the day.

Move Every Hour

Even if you're working or studying, take a 5-minute break every hour to stretch or walk around. It can improve focus and overall health.

Healthy Snacking

Instead of sugary snacks, opt for fruits, nuts, or yogurt. They're healthy and keep your energy levels steady.
